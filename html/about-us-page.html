    About Willify

    Willify isn't just about listening to music; it's about discovering new favorites and revisiting old classics. Explore our extensive catalog spanning millions of songs and podcasts. We personalize your experience with custom playlists and recommendations based on your listening habits. Willify stands out for its commitment to a seamless and enjoyable music experience. We leverage cutting-edge streaming technology to ensure high-quality audio, whether you're discovering new tunes or jamming to your favorite oldies.

    Willify History

    Willify is a leading music streaming platform that's been bringing the world closer to music since 2006. We started as a simple digital audio service, but our passion for music fueled our growth into a global phenomenon with millions of active users. Our founders, a group of passionate music lovers who envisioned a platform that would: Offer a vast and diverse music library for everyone, make music discovery easier and more enjoyable, provide a seamless and high-quality listening experience. With this vision in mind, Willify was born.

    Willify Founders

    Willify's story began in 2006 with the vision of a group of passionate music lovers: Luci Margaret, Catherine Route, and Avalui Warney. Back then, music accessibility wasn't what it is today. Physical media was dominant, and digital options were fragmented and inconvenient. Avalui dreamt of a platform that offered easy access to a wider range of music without breaking the bank. Luci saw a gap in the market for a user-friendly streaming service that catered to niche interests and fostered music discovery. Catherine envisioned a platform that empowered listeners and rekindled the joy of exploring new music.

    About Our Founders

    Luci Margaret

    Luci Margaret's fascination with technology began at a young age. Surrounded by computers and gadgets in a tech-enthusiast family, Luci developed a knack for problem-solving and a love for tinkering. She channeled her passion into a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from MIT, graduating with skills that were highly sought after in the software development world. Yet, Luci's true passion resided in the world of music, particularly in the vibrant underground scenes that thrived just beneath the mainstream radar.

    Catherine Route

    Catherine Route spent years immersed in the world of music journalism, crafting insightful articles and reviews as a Master's graduate from Columbia University's Graduate School of Journalism. Witnessing the industry shift towards profit-driven metrics left her concerned about a growing disconnect between artists and fans. Fueled by a lifelong appreciation for music, instilled in her by her family's diverse musical interests, Catherine sought to rekindle the joy of music exploration and foster a community where music could truly connect.

    Avalui Warney

    Avalui Warney's life revolved around music. Growing up in a family where both parents were music teachers, he was nurtured on the discipline and beauty of classical music. After graduating with a Master's in Violin Performance from Juilliard, he pursued her passion professionally, performing with local orchestras and chamber groups. However, her love for music extended beyond the confines of classical repertoire eventually lead her to deveelop Willify.

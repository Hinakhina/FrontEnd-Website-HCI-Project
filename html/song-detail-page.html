"If One Stop Heart From Breaking"
By: Robin - HoyoMix
A song from Honkai Star Rail Version 2.0, one of Hoyoverse games. The song's melancholic piano melody and soft vocals capture the ache of heartbreak, but with a glimmer of hope. The lyrics explore the desire to shield someone from emotional pain, finding purpose and meaning in offering comfort and support.
